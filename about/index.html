About



It’s hard being a solo founder.

Most startups fail. Even more solo founder startups fail.

It's no wonder why...

  • Mental Health - you're going alone, against the grain
  • Resources - you have ½ or ⅓ the resources of most founding teams
  • Skills - limited skillset in founding team
  • Ideation - no co-founder to bounce ideas off of
  • Feedback - no one going through the same process who can give you detailed feedback
  • Decision making - no one to stop you making bad decisions
  • It's less fun - there's no one who truly understands and is there to cheer you up when things go wrong
  • Accountability - no co-founder relying on you / to keep you in check
  • Expensive - slower, paying for limitations
  • Investment - investors less likely to invest

We're tackling these problems with Founder Tribe.

Founder Tribe brings together talented solo founders with shared values.

Fellow tribe members help solve each other's problems, meeting regularly in London at our events and remotely too.

Founder Tribe creates tools, mechanisms, frameworks and processes to ensure the tribe's success.

Run by Maxxwell Firth; made valuable by the many more who attend, contribute and share their experiences and wisdom with the rest of the community.

We currently grow through recommendations from our strong community. Have someone to recommend or want to get in touch with us about something else? Email maxxwell@foundertribe.org.




Our Values



  1. Community

    2. Be nice. Relationships matter and collaboration is key. Give and get back to and from your fellow tribe members.

  2. Execution

    3. Ideas are worthless. What's important? Getting stuff done and figuring stuff out.

  3. Iteration

    4. Keep evolving your idea in response to customer feedback, not sure what to do? Speak to your customers.

  4. Vulnerability

    5. Awareness of your weaknesses is just as important as awareness of your strengths.

  5. Diversity

    6. Different backgrounds, different ways of thinking, different networks; a strong tribe is a diverse tribe.
