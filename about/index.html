About



Founder Tribe brings together collaborative solo founders at fast-growing businesses.

Questions are asked and answered by the community over email and slack.

We meet regularly in London to discuss specific issues the group is currently facing.

Run by Maxxwell Firth; made valuable by the many more who attend, contribute and share their experiences and wisdom with the rest of the community.

We currently grow through recommendations from our strong community. Have someone to recommend or want to get in touch with us about something else? Email maxxwell@foundertribe.org.
